STAB Clan History

STAB Clan began as a small group of competitive knifers in 2009, built on precision, discipline, and a love for the craft. What started as a handful of players quickly evolved into a community defined by loyalty, skill, and a legacy that spans more than a decade. Through every era, every game, and every challenge, STAB has remained committed to excellence — not just in gameplay, but in the bonds formed along the way.

From legendary OGs to rising talent, STAB continues to grow while honoring the players who shaped its identity. This timeline documents the journey — year by year — from our earliest days to the present.
